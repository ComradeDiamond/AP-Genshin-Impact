About the Course

This course will challenge you in the knowledge of Teyvat, the people, the lands, the cultures, and most importantly, the journey.


Skills You'll Learn

  • Analyzing calculations, equations, and nuances of interactions involving enemy and character stats

  • Optimizing, evaluating, and crafting team compositions

  • Putting historical developments in context and making connections between them

  • Explaining the impact and implications of each archon's interpretations of the "heavenly principles"

  • Reading and analyzing text and visual sources

  • Developing a claim or thesis and supporting it in an essay

Equivalency and Prerequisites

  • College Course Equivalent

    A one-semester introductory college course in comparative government and politics

  • Recommended Prerequisites

    None
